Hi, I’m Clément Gautrais, CTO at Brightclue. I work on developping a search engine for 3D data. I am no longer working in academia, but I am still interested in any partnership related to my current job.

I was a Post-doctoral researcher in the DTAI research group at KU Leuven. I mostly worked on the SYNTH project, under the supervision of Luc De Raedt. My main research focus was on developing tools to automate data science. My main research expertise is on pattern mining.

I did my PhD thesis at the IRISA Lab in the LACODAM team. The subject of my thesis was “Detection and characterization of user behavior in a Big Data context: application to complex rhythms study in retail.” My supervisors were Alexandre Termier, Peggy Cellier, Thomas Guyet and René Quiniou.
